SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Strategic location in a key economic hub (Dong Xuyen Industrial Park).

  • Stable market from heavy industries (oil & gas, power, steel).
  • High internal demand from local sectors like logistics and shipbuilding.
  • Well-trained and highly experienced workforce in specialized steel cables.

Weaknesses

  • Vulnerable to economic cycles affecting import/export and investment.
  • Intense price competition, especially from smaller businesses.
  • Dependent on the parent company for raw material supply and pricing.
  • Capital tied up in a large, hard-to-liquidate inventory of old cables.
  • Business strategy lacks sufficient support and investment.
  • Parent company's business philosophy mismatches Vietnamese business culture.

Opportunities

  • Potential to become an official supplier for major corporations in shipbuilding, construction, etc.

Threats

  • Fierce competition from low-priced domestic and international suppliers (e.g., China).
